About.

The first project of seattle.io is to research local topics in Seattle, WA, and compile what we learn into digital books.

Each book will include interviews with local folks about what they do, why they do it, and how they do it. In addition, each book will include how-to guides for getting involved with that topic, and a comprehensive directory for learning more about the topic.

We also bundle each book with related items. For instance, with the book about local indie game developers we'll bundle the book with games from some of those developers.


Goals of seattle.io:

  • Build a community of entrepreneurs and technologists focused on journalism and publishing in Seattle.
  • Create in-depth resources serving residents of Seattle and the surrounding area.
  • Experiment with the intersection of storytelling and technology.
  • Support the development of open-source technology and the open-source community in Seattle.
  • Develop sustainable business and publishing models for journalism that can be replicated in other communities.
