Play Local: exploring games made in Seattle.

The book will include interviews with local indie game developers, how-to guides for getting started as an indie game developer, and a comprehensive directory to all the indie video game things in the Seattle area. We'll also offer the book bundled with some of the games made by local indie developers!

Do you live in Seattle and make video games? We should interview you! Send us an email at hi@seattle.io

Games on SeattleWiki

As we research the games being made in Seattle, we use SeattleWiki as a kind of "open reporter's notebook".

You can follow along with new info, and even add info about your own games or games made in Seattle that you know about by visiting seattlewiki.net/games and editing that page!
