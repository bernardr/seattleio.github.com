Events & Workshops.

seattle.io hosts a number of workshops, hackathons, and other events focused on the intersection of journalism, storytelling, and technology. Keep updated on events by subscribing to our newsletter and by joining our group on meetup.com.

workshops

Learn to code

A 3-hour introduction to a technology like javascript or ruby.

The next learn to code workshop is Introduction to Creative Coding with Javascript on Monday, December 2.

Hacker series

A series of 3 4-hour workshops that provide a deep dive into a technology.

Possible topics: javascript, ruby, python, wordpress, ghost, node.js

Blog locally: creating a community blog

A 4-hour workshop on creating and maintaining a locally-focused news blog.

Basics of journalism

A 2-hour workshop covering the basics of journalism.

seattle.io editorial meetup

Interested in becoming a contributor? Want to learn more about seattle.io? Working on your own local blog and want to share notes and possibly collaborate with other local journalists?

First hour is seattle.io editorial planning overview. Remaining time is open for eating snacks and discussing local media.
